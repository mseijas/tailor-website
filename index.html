The smart closet

Automatically tracks your clothes

Tailor uses miniature sensors embedded in your clothing to detect the items you own and track the garments you wear on a daily basis. All without requiring any user input.

Personalized outfit suggestions

Tailor's powerful artificial intelligence agent learns from you to understand your style and what you like to wear. Tailor helps you get dressed in the morning by offering you outfit suggestions that adjust to your preferences and are appropriate for the day's weather conditions.

Explore your closet digitally

Tailor's intuitive user interface allows you to search, filter, and explore your closet from the comfort of your couch or even while you're away.
